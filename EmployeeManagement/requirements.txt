Employee Types:

Full-Time Employee: Has a fixed monthly salary.

Part-Time Employee: Paid based on the number of hours worked and an hourly rate.

Intern: Receives a fixed stipend, regardless of hours worked.

Functionality:

Each employee should have a calculateSalary() method that calculates their salary based on their type.

The system should allow adding employees and displaying their details (name, type, and calculated salary).
